Biography

Born June 21, 1988, in Lagos Nigeria. His musical journey began in 1999 when he joined forces with a partner, making a notable impact on the Nigerian music scene by 2009 with collaborations with industry figures like PM (now known as Password), Indomix, and PerfPerfSoundz. In 2011, Johnny Webb chose to pursue a solo career, marking the start of a focused effort to make his mark on the Nigerian music scene. His solo debut in 2013 included singles Drink In Ma Cup and Redi, featuring Skuki. In 2015, Webb took a temporary break from music to pursue a business management degree, broadening his knowledge and skills. This break prepared him for a successful return to the music scene in 2018, where he founded Webb Recordz, showcasing his entrepreneurial spirit. The label released an album titled 'The Exposure' and a single, "Human Life" featuring Lou-is, an African-American rapper from Columbus, Ohio.
