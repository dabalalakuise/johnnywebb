Nothing is Impossible
Single Release

Certainly, here's a revised version: "Nothing Impossible" is an inspirational song that ignites hope, guiding individuals from the brink of despair to a new phase where the impossible transforms into reality. Ejimgi
Single Release

"Ejimgi" is a heartfelt love song that delves into the essence of love itself, exploring its origins, the anticipation of the future, and all that it encompasses. This powerful composition serves as a soothing balm for those nursing a broken heart, while also paving the way for individuals who are embarking on their journey of belief in love.

The Exposure
EP/Album

"The Exposure" stands as an exquisite piece of Afrobeat crafted by the talented John Ejimchi Uche. This musical compilation comprises six dynamic Afro tracks, offering a captivating total recording duration of 21.25 minutes. The tracklist includes the engaging titles of Famous, Morale, Mbo, Confidence, Human Life, and Pikin.
